# Inflexions Academy -- Strategic Proposal

**Prepared for**: Inflexions IT Board of Directors
**Date**: April 2026
**Classification**: Internal -- For Decision

---

## Executive Summary

We propose launching **Inflexions Academy** -- a structured, expert-led training arm that monetises the deep domain expertise already resident within the company. The Academy targets two revenue streams: **open-enrolment programmes** for individual professionals and **enterprise training engagements** for corporate teams and institutions.

This is not a pivot. It is a natural extension of what Inflexions already does. Every solution we deploy, every system we manage, and every advisory engagement we deliver is underpinned by specialised knowledge. The Academy packages that knowledge into a repeatable, scalable, high-margin offering.

---

## The Strategic Case

### Why Training, Why Now

1. **Skills gap is widening**: AI adoption, cloud migration, and regulatory pressure (ISO 27001, data protection) are creating urgent demand for structured upskilling across West Africa and beyond.

2. **High-margin, recurring revenue**: Training programmes have 60-80% gross margins once developed. Unlike project work, they can be delivered repeatedly with minimal incremental cost.

3. **Pipeline generation**: A CTO who sends 10 engineers to an Inflexions cloud course will think of Inflexions when the cloud migration RFP lands. Training is the top of the consulting funnel.

4. **Competitive moat**: Most IT vendors in the region are resellers or implementers. An academy positions Inflexions as a *knowledge leader* -- a category of one.

5. **Talent retention**: Giving senior staff a teaching mandate adds variety to their roles and positions them as industry authorities.

### What We Are NOT Building

- Not an online learning platform (no LMS, no video hosting).
- Not a university or degree programme.
- Not dependent on any single individual's availability.

We are building a **marketed, structured catalogue of expert-led training programmes** delivered in-person, virtually, or on-site at client premises. The website drives enquiries. Delivery happens in the real world.

## The Offering Architecture

### Brand: Inflexions Academy

"Academy" signals rigour, structure, and institutional credibility. It mirrors established industry precedents (Cisco Networking Academy, AWS Academy, Google Cloud Skills Boost) while remaining distinctly ours.

### Four Competency Domains

Training is organised into domains -- not around people. This ensures the Academy scales as the team grows. Any qualified instructor can deliver within a domain.

| Domain | What It Covers | Market Signal |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| **AI & Intelligent Systems** | AI fundamentals, LLM engineering, AI for industry verticals (healthcare, finance, marketing, education), AI-era business strategy | Hottest growth area; every executive wants AI literacy |
| **Infrastructure & Cloud** | Network architecture, data centre design, HCI, virtualisation, containerisation, AWS/GCP/Azure, Linux & Windows Server | Foundational; steady corporate demand |
| **Cybersecurity & Compliance** | InfoSec (all levels), GRC, ISO 27001, CISA/CISM prep, security architecture, auditing | Regulatory-driven; non-discretionary spend |
| **Digital Strategy & Transformation** | Marketing & digital comms, startup strategy, enterprise digital transformation, data-driven decision-making | Cross-cuts all industries; appeals to executives |

### Two Audience Segments

| Segment | Who They Are | What They Buy | How They Find Us |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Individuals** | IT professionals, career changers, entrepreneurs | Open-enrolment programmes, certification prep | Website, LinkedIn, word of mouth |
| **Organisations** | Corporate L&D teams, HR departments, government agencies | Custom team training, on-site delivery, volume packages | Direct sales, website enquiry form, existing client relationships |

## Go-to-Market Integration

### Website Presence

The Academy will have **full visibility** across the Inflexions website:

1. **Main Navigation**: "Academy" appears as a dropdown menu item alongside Solutions and Services -- equal prominence.
2. **Dedicated Section**: A full `/academy` section with:
   - Academy landing page (overview of all domains)
   - Individual domain pages (programme listings per domain)
   - Programme detail pages (curriculum, objectives, registration CTA)
   - Corporate training page (enterprise enquiry form, custom training process)
3. **Homepage Feature**: A dedicated "Develop Your Edge" section on the homepage introducing the Academy.
4. **Cross-linking**: Every Solution page (Cloud, Security, Network, Data) links to related training programmes, creating a natural bridge between "what we build" and "what we teach."
5. **Footer**: Academy link added to site-wide navigation.

## What We Need to Decide

The following decisions require director input before implementation begins:

### 1. Pricing Strategy
- What price range per programme for individuals?
- What's the corporate pricing model (per head? per day? flat fee)?
- Do we offer early-bird or volume discounts?

### 2. Delivery Logistics
- Where will in-person programmes be delivered? (Inflexions office, rented venues, client sites?)
- What virtual platform? (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet?)
- What's the minimum class size to run a programme?

### 3. Certification & Accreditation
- Will we issue Inflexions-branded certificates of completion?
- Do we pursue partnership with certification bodies (e.g., PECB for ISO 27001)?
- Any plans for CPE/CPD credit recognition?

### 4. Content Development
- Who leads curriculum development for each domain?
- What's the timeline for having the initial 10 programmes ready to deliver?
- Do we invest in branded course materials (printed workbooks, slide decks)?

### 5. Sales & Marketing
- Who owns the Academy P&L?
- Do we market through existing channels or create separate Academy social profiles?
- Do we target specific verticals first (e.g., financial services for InfoSec, healthcare for AI)?
